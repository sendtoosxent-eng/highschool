COMMUNE HIGH SCHOOL

Commune High School, established in February 2019 by experienced educationists in collaboration with the community, embodies the principle of providing equal educational opportunities to both boys and girls from disadvantaged backgrounds. The school’s name reflects this commitment to inclusivity and community involvement. Commune High School offers a competency-based curriculum designed to ensure academic excellence and holistic development. In addition to this rigorous academic program, the school provides vocational skills training. This dual approach prepares students not only for academic success but also equips them with practical skills. This is particularly beneficial for those who may need to leave school due to financial constraints, as it enables them to secure viable employment opportunities and support themselves and their families.

Through its innovative and inclusive educational model, Commune High School strives to empower students from disadvantaged communities, fostering both academic and vocational growth to ensure a brighter future for all its students.
